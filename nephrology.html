Renel Sciences


With the sedentary lifestyles and unhealthy dietary habits of the growing population, kidneys tend to be the organs that are most affected primarily. Chronic kidney disease (CKD) and acute kidney injury are on the rise worldwide. CKD affects as many as 10-15% of the world’s population and is associated with accelerated aging and severe cardiovascular complications. Inadequate hydration, heat stress due to global warming, a sedentary lifestyle and overuse of protein pump inhibitors can contribute to this epidemic. The Department of Nephrology at Star Hospital offers top-notch medical services to conquer the wide spectrum of adult and pediatric kidney diseases. We analyze the root causal factor of the disorder; indulge in pre-operative evaluation, dialysis support, and complete post-operative care by channelizing our efforts to prohibit the chance of recurrence.
Urology is a surgical specialty involved in the management of disorders involving the genitourinary system, i.e. Kidneys, Ureters, Urinary bladder, Prostate and Male Genitalia – Penis and Testicles. Urological disorders are increasing due to various factors like westernization of dietary habits, smoking, alcoholism, stress, etc. These disorders can be seen across all age groups and genders. Miniaturisation of endoscopic equipment, availability of lasers, laparoscopic equipment and advanced gadgets have revolutionized the treatment modalities in the field of urology. The Department of Urology at Star Hospital offers special packages tailored to different age, gender and risk groups. With state of the art technology and surgical expertise, this renowned hospital is in the forefront for providing world-class urological care to the needy at a very affordable cost.

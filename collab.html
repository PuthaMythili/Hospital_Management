Our Collaborations


Our hospitals offer various healthcare programs for individual employees from corporate houses and provide tangible benefits to the organisation as a whole. It is aimed at facilitating impactful research activities focused on creating new and better ways to treat patients and has published papers in many national and international journals. We invite research institutions, organisations & collaborations with pharmaceutical and health insurance companies to join us in various areas. We strive to provide better and evolved healthcare services to our patients. From collaborative research to the exchange of technological know-how. Our activities span from clinical trials to testing and development of specific treatments for cardiac diseases. There are many ways you can help support STAR Hospital with tax-deductible donations. Benefactor recognition areas include the Halls of Benefactors, recognition walls, and display boards in our hospitals.

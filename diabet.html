Diabetology & Internal Medicine


The department of Internal Medicine at STAR Hospitals provides comprehensive primary and specialty care to treat a wide array of potential diseases. The doctors are well trained from India and abroad with significant experience in treating patients transcending all age groups, diagnosed with complex medical problems. The department’s focus is to provide suitable care for people with both acute and chronic illnesses such as Hypertension, High Cholesterol, Diabetes and other metabolic problems by providing viable solutions with significant results.

Our primary focus is to deliver uncompromised quality care with highest compassion through a team of highly skilled doctors. Touted as a department where each and every patient is treated with dignity and respect, the team provides close outpatient follow-ups and is available round the clock. It is rightly said that “Prevention is Better than Cure “. Apart from focusing on early diagnosis and treatment, we drive our efforts towards creating awareness among the people about the vagaries of diabetes and actions to be taken to circumvent it.

We provide unrelenting services in managing patients with diabetes, as well as those with pre-diabetes, metabolic syndrome and insulin resistance. By focusing on lifestyle interventions and employing the latest medications and technologies to treat or prevent diabetes, STAR Hospitals seek to improve the health and well-being of all of its patients. Our goal is to give our patients with diabetes-related metabolic conditions the necessary tools to successfully self-manage the day-to-day rigours of life. Our program aims to form a sense of harmony with our patients to meet their needs and give them the best diabetes care available.
